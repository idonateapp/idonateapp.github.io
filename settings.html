Where are the Settings?

The Short Answer: They're coming soon

Settings

As of right now, due to time constraints on the challenge, there are no settings in the app.

Planned Settings

We already have a few settings we plan to add.

  1. Customized Notes: We plan to allow the users to leave a customized note with each donation
  2. Local Charities: We plan to use the users location to find charities nearby to speed up the charity finding process.
  3. iTunes Purchasing: We plan to allow the users to use their iTunes funds to purchase their gift, as opposed to their Amazon account
